# AI Impact on Jobs 2030 | Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the impact of Artificial Intelligence on jobs, salaries, workforce demand, and automation risk across various industries using Power BI.

The dashboard provides interactive insights into AI replacement risk, salary trends, future job demand, job satisfaction, and workforce characteristics.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze AI replacement risk across industries and job roles.
* Examine salary trends in different sectors.
* Study future job demand.
* Evaluate job satisfaction levels.
* Understand workforce characteristics through experience and education levels.

---

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX
* Data Visualization
* Interactive Slicers

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Average Salary: 126.64K USD
